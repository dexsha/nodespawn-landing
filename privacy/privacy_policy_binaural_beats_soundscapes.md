# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how **Blaž Primožič, s.p.**, Trg komandanta Staneta 8, 1000 Ljubljana, Slovenia (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile application **Binaural Beats Soundscapes** (the “App”).

If you have any questions, you can contact us at:
**[dev@nodespawn.com](mailto:dev@nodespawn.com)**

## 2. Data Safety Summary

We value transparency and user privacy.

* We **collect and share certain data** with third-party services to operate and improve the App.
* Data is used for **analytics, advertising, app functionality, and purchases**.
* Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
* Certain data collection is necessary for core app functionality, while other data (such as personalized advertising) is optional and based on user consent.
* Users can **request deletion of their data** at any time (see Section 12).

## 3. Information We Collect

We and our third-party service providers may collect the following types of data:

### a) Device and Technical Information

* Device model
* Operating system
* Device identifiers (including advertising ID)

### b) Usage Data

* App interactions
* Features used
* Session duration

### c) Location Information

* Approximate location derived from IP address
* **We do not collect precise location data**

### d) Advertising Data

* Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)

### e) Purchase Information

* Anonymous user identifiers
* Purchase history and transaction status

**We do not directly collect or store payment information. Payments are processed by the platform provider (e.g., Google Play).**

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

* Provide and operate the App
* Improve app performance and features
* Analyze usage and user behavior
* Display and optimize advertisements
* Manage in-app purchases
* Ensure security and prevent misuse

## 7. Advertising

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect:

* Advertising identifiers
* Device information
* IP address and approximate location
* Ad interaction data

### Personalized Ads

Where permitted, we may show **personalized ads** based on user data.

Users can:

* Provide or withdraw consent via in-app consent prompts (EEA users)

## 8. Analytics

We use **Firebase** to understand how users interact with the App.

This includes:

* Features used
* Time spent in the App
* Crash reports and performance data

We use consent signals to control analytics data collection in accordance with applicable laws. Where required, analytics storage and processing may depend on user consent, and may be limited if consent is not provided.

Consent is obtained via in-app consent prompts and can be withdrawn at any time.

## 9. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are managed by **RevenueCat**.

RevenueCat may process:

* Anonymous user identifiers

* Transaction status

Payments are securely handled by the platform provider (e.g., Google Play).

## 10. Children’s Privacy

The App is **not directed to children under 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children, and we do not knowingly serve personalized ads to users identified as children.

## 14. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your data by contacting:

**[dev@nodespawn.com](mailto:dev@nodespawn.com)**

### How to request deletion
Please include:
- The app name (**Binaural Beats Soundscapes**)  
- Any relevant identifying details (e.g., device information)

### What data is deleted
Upon request, we will take reasonable steps to delete data associated with your device collected through the app, including:

- **Analytics data** (such as app usage events and interactions) collected via Firebase Analytics  
- **Crash and diagnostic data** (such as crash logs, device type, and operating system version) collected via Firebase Crashlytics  
- **Advertising-related data** (such as device identifiers like Advertising ID) used by AdMob for ad delivery and personalization, where applicable  
- **Purchase and subscription data** (such as transaction history and anonymous user identifiers) processed via RevenueCat  

### Important notes

- This app does not use user accounts. Data is associated with your device and may not always be individually identifiable  
- Some data may be processed and stored by third-party providers (such as Google and RevenueCat), and deletion of such data may require action in accordance with their respective privacy policies
- Users can reset their advertising identifier in device settings to limit further data collection for advertising purposes  
- Certain data may be retained where required by law, for fraud prevention, or for financial record-keeping  

### Processing time
We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days).
